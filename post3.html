Progress

New challenges are coming and our focus right now is how to solve theese particular tasks.

Posted by Veronica Hage on Monday May 10, 2019

After looking more closely at the model we found for simulating liquid-filled models we’ve come to the realization that we can’t use it as it would make our project very limited for further development. We decided to use a particle system instead which will make the project more open for creativity. We started to experiment with Unity’s built-in particle system, adding gravity and making the particles collide with other objects to somewhat mimic running water. After further research we found the concept of metaballs and found a script for this on Unity’s own wiki-page. We’ve discussed to try and simulate a lavalamp. This could start with creating blobs moving up and down and add-ons could be: - Simulating the liquids interacting with the lamps bottom and roof - Simulating heat which could change the behavior/density of the liquids.

We want to develop a great project with different extension depending on our resources and our time.
