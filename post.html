Writing our specification

Today we are writing our specification.

Posted by Sabina von Essen on Monday April 29, 2019

With density, one expresses how large a given volume of a substance is. Small density causes bodies to float, high density causes bodies to sink. In the field of chemistry in primary and secondary school, students learn how different liquids and densities behave towards each other. A visual program to demonstrate this could lead to a better understanding of different densities among young students. This could also motivate and encourage students to learn in terms of a more fun education. When starting up the template the project looked like this: By using this tool, we will start off by writing our own shaderScripts for different liquids and trying to fill up an object with these different liquids. The first challenge will therefore be to modify the example below to hold several liquids in the same model. We will then work through our extensions and see how far we are able to manage our goals.
